The individual and IT

Reflection DISC-test

In the context of iTalent, we had to take the DISC test at the beginning of the semester. Here we briefly reflect on the result.
With me 'Influence' and 'Dominant' clearly come forward. This is something I also expected, I am indeed a person who likes to have it according to my wishes.

“You are socially oriented. You have an intrinsic motivation to get to know people from very different backgrounds and to maintain these relationships well.
You have a natural enthusiasm for all kinds of ideas and developments, both for yourself and for others.
People probably think you are social, convincing and optimistic.”

Above you can read the explanation about my results. I largely agree with this, except for one thing. I do get to know a lot of different people, only maintaining relationships is sometimes more difficult for me.

I largely agree with the rest. It is true that I like to push good ideas forward and that I like to involve as much people with it as I can.
