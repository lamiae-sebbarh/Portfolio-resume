VRelive

An innovative IT-related concept

For the group assignment 'InnovIT' we had to devise an innovative IT-related concept.
We could choose the concept ourselfs. And this without any restrictions, except that it could not already exist. With our group of six we came up with the idea of the VRelive glasses. The initial idea can be found in the document below ("iRelive - the idea").
We also made mock-ups, a commercial video and a fictional website. We divided these tasks among us six. From these tasks I took care of the mock-ups, together with the help of Jelle. In addition to that, everyone helped eachother to search for errors or improvements. Or of course to say that it all looked very good!

This way we also won the InnovIT awards in our subgroup. All groups were divided into three subgroups, of which we belonged to the third subgroup.
This was a pleasant surprise after such a good work.
